Granny Smith

A tart treat.


Granny Smith are another apple native to Australia. Discovered as a chance seedling, Granny Smith have been cultivated in commerical orchards for nearly 150 years. Many consumers love Granny Smith and know it as the original sour apple. The dense flesh and tangy, acidic flavors make Granny Smith a powerhouse of flavors. Granny Smith is one of the most popular cooking apples in the United States, with sales peaking at Thanksgiving. It's flavors seem to be enhanced through cooking making it a popular choice for holiday pies, sauces and other apple treats.

  1. Available October through July.

  2. Sweet to Tart Scale: 10/10.

  3. Granny Smith is named after the Australian apple grower who discovered it.
